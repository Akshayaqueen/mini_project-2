Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Dark Knight - A thrilling superhero film that explores moral dilemmas and Gotham's descent into chaos.
  2. Inception - A mind-bending exploration of dreams within dreams, led by a stellar performance from Leonardo DiCaprio.
  3. Interstellar - An epic journey across space and time with stunning visuals and an emotional core.
  4. The Matrix - A groundbreaking science fiction film that questions reality and the power of human choice.
  5. Fight Club - A dark, thought-provoking film about identity, masculinity, and rebellion against consumerism.
